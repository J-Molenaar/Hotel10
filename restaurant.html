Dine with Us It'll blow your mind.

Choose from our 3 famous restaurants. Each with it's own theme, whichever you choose, you won't be disappointed. Don't want to leave your room? No worries, we will bring you what you want, when you want.

Have a Drink Wines From Around the World

The Wine Cellar is the best kept secret; a prohibition era-style cocktail lounge with a vintage two-lane bowling alley and custom-made wooden board games. Have a bottle from our private wine cellar featuring wines from around the world. With over 1,000 bottles, we have a wine for any taste.

Stay In 24 Hour In-Room Dining:

Stay cozy and eat anytime you want with our 24 hour in-room dining. The select menu includes all-day brunch items from Main Stays Cafe and diner-style fare from 425 Degrees.

Splash Pool Side Burger Bar

Indulge in American cafe fare and cocktails beside the renowned pool. Food and drinks at your table or delivered to your lounge chair.

HOURS

Daily 10am - 2pm

The Game Room

The Game Room is a gaming parlor and cocktail lounge, built on social interaction that brings groups together through their appreciation and passion for innovative cocktails, small plates, music, design, and the camaraderie of gaming.

HOURS

Daily 3pm - 2pm

Roosevelt Steak House

Visit the critically-acclaimed Roosevelt Steak House. Enjoy handcrafted cocktails, day-boat oysters, charcuterie and artisanal cheeses, expertly-roasted chicken, or our signature steaks served with potatoes and topped with house made caramelized shallot bordelaise.

HOURS

Daily 5pm - 10:30pm
